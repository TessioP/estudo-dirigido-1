\frametitle{Maxwell's Equations in Cartesian Coordinates}
The Maxwell's Equations are:
\begin{align*}
    \rot \Et(t) &= -\partial _t \Bt(t), \\
    \rot \Hht(t) &= \partial_t \Dt(t), \\
    \Div \Bt(t) &= 0, \\
    \Div \Dt(t) &= 0,
\end{align*}
where $\partial_t\cdot = \partialDerivative{\cdot}{t}$.
